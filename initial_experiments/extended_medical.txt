flu is commonly associated with fever, cough, sore throat, fatigue, body aches and chills.

covid19 often presents with fever, dry cough, loss of taste, shortness of breath, fatigue, headache and muscle pain.

malaria typically leads to fever, chills, sweating, headache, nausea and vomiting.

diabetes is characterized by frequent urination, excessive thirst, fatigue, blurred vision and slow healing wounds.

migraine is linked to severe headache, nausea, sensitivity to light, vomiting, dizziness and visual disturbance.

asthma is associated with shortness of breath, wheezing, chest tightness and difficulty breathing during exercise.

pneumonia often causes fever, cough, chest pain, difficulty breathing, fatigue and sweating.

tuberculosis presents with persistent cough, fever, weight loss, night sweats, chest pain and fatigue.

hypertension may lead to headache, dizziness, blurred vision, chest pain and shortness of breath.

gastritis is commonly linked to stomach pain, nausea, bloating, vomiting and loss of appetite.

anemia is characterized by fatigue, weakness, dizziness, pale skin and shortness of breath.

chickenpox typically causes fever, itchy rash, fatigue, loss of appetite and red spots on skin.

hepatitis is associated with jaundice, fatigue, abdominal pain, nausea, dark urine and loss of appetite.
